### Referral Management Product Details
- **Problem**: Healthcare organizations lose 55%-65% of patient referrals to out-of-network providers
- **Revenue Impact**: Up to $1.54M lost per employed PCP annually
- **Conversion Issue**: Only 54% of faxed referrals lead to appointments
- **Patient Impact**: 46% of patients don't receive the care they need
- **Solution**: Automates referrals, reduces leakage, improves communication
- **Target**: Built for U.S. health plans and TPAs
- **Integration**: Connects with claims, clinical, and EMPI systems

### Appeals & Grievances Product Details
- **Target**: U.S. health plans and TPAs
- **Key Features**:
  - Enforces CMS timeliness rules
  - Generates regulator-grade notices
  - Maintains complete audit trail
  - Automated case routing with SLA timers
  - Real-time dashboards for timeliness, backlog, and overturn trends
  - Now Assist automates validation using knowledge articles
- **Benefits**: Reduces compliance risk, faster resolution, protects member trust
